About the Evening

Niigata makes more sake than any prefecture except Hyōgo. It also makes a particular kind of sake — clean, dry, mineral, shaped by snowmelt water and a colder, slower fermentation than most of Japan.

Five pours, light to heavy, with seasonal dishes from Hashida's counter alongside. Kenji locks in the final lineup the week of the event based on what's landed fresh — which is the whole point of drinking sake this way.
