The Brewery
Hakkaisan Sake Brewery
View
  • Niigata Prefecture — Established 1922
  • Hakkaisan is located in one of the snowiest regions in Japan, where heavy snowfall accumulates in winter and melts in spring, filtering through mountain soil to create pure and soft water.
  • The key philosophies of our brewery encompass small batch production, hand-made koji, and long, slow, cold fermentations, which result in the coveted "tanrei karakuchi" style sake—light, clean, and dry with a short finish.
