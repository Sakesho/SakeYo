Tried this warmed at Orihara last night.

The yamahai funk lands different at 45°C — almost like a dry sherry. The acidity that feels sharp cold turns into something rounder, almost savoury. Not for a first date, but exactly right for a cold evening.

Paired it with grilled mackerel and pickled daikon. Yamagami-san's old advice from Shu Shu finally clicks: heavy sake, oily fish, warmed.
